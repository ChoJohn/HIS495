FAQ

How do I register for UC Orientation?

You can register online for Orientation at ucorientation.com/register. Payments online are accepted in CREDIT only. If you cannot pay online via credit, please contact orientation.registration@uclit.ca to set up an alternate payment method!

When can I register?

You can pre-register from now up until August 22nd. Registration is also open September 5th (the first day of orientation) however we encourage pre-registration, as spaces on the day-of are limited!

What is Orientation?

UC Orientation will be the best week of your university experience! Filled with unimaginable fun activities along with very informative interactive info sessions, Orientation week is a great opportunity to make many new friends in the community and get acquainted with college spaces and values! It will allow you to meet other students in your program, ask questions to upper year students and faculty, and get to know the campus before your classes start. It also introduces you to the city of Toronto, encouraging you to explore your new home.

Why should I attend Orientation?

UC Orientation helps to set you up for your first year. It connects you with resources and support systems, introduces you to your college and student spaces, and creates friendships and communities for the school year. UC Orientation helps facilitate your academic transition and prepare you for your first year, as well as making this U of T feel a little more like home!

If I’m a commuter, can I still attend Orientation?

Yes! UC Orientation is for both on and off campus students. The week also features a commuter meet-up event, so that you can connect with people you’ll be commuting with!

I have a long commute to campus from home - can I stay on campus during the week to avoid commuting?

We have a billeting program, which connects commuter students with residence students who are able provide accommodations for the week. If you are a commuter and you are interested in the billeting program, you can sign up here: _________ NEED TO GET THIS FROM JOANNA. The deadline for billeting applications is August 24th. Applications are processed on a first-come-first-serve basis.

What do I bring to Orientation?

Before each day, remember to check the schedule to ensure you bring the proper attire for the events, and are prepared for different weather scenarios. That said, please bring sunscreen, a hat and a rain jacket. Also, make sure you bring adequate footwear! Running shoes and general athletic wear are recommended for many high-energy events. The “Energy Level” ratings provided on the schedule will help you to recognize these events. You are provided with one t-shirt, which you are required to wear to all UC Orientation events, except the semi-formal Red and White Ball. If you are a commuter billeting throughout the week, we highly recommend that you bring a sleeping bag and pillow.

Will my dietary restrictions/allergies be accommodated for?

Make sure to include ALL of your allergies and dietary restrictions when you complete your registration form so that our meals team may accommodate for them!

I require financial assistance to attend Orientation, is that possible?

University College does offer students in need with financial assistance in order to facilitate the attendance of Orientation. However, bursaries for UC Orientation are limited in number. If you are in financial need and wish to attend UC Orientation, please email orientation@uclit.ca as soon as possible!
